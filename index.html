Questions? Answered.

How is the Revenue calculated?

Our engine uses a proprietary algorithm that multiplies daily views by industry-standard RPM (Revenue Per Mille) rates. You can adjust the RPM based on your niche to get a highly accurate projection of earnings.

Is CalcGrow secure and private?

Absolutely. CalcGrow is a 100% independent and secure platform. We use public API data to fetch metrics. We never ask for your passwords, login credentials, or private analytics access. Privacy is built-in.

Do I need a subscription?

No. The core utility tools on CalcGrow are currently free for creators globally. Our goal is to empower digital entrepreneurs with transparent data without subscription paywalls.
