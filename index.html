How exactly does CalcGrow predict revenue?

Our engine utilizes a proprietary algorithm that cross-references daily view counts with industry-standard RPMs from tier-1, tier-2, and tier-3 countries to provide a highly accurate estimation.

Do I need to link my YouTube account?

No. CalcGrow operates on a strict privacy-first architecture. We only use public data inputs provided by you. No OAuth logins or permissions are required.

Are these tools free to use?

Yes, all basic RPM and revenue calculators are completely free to use for creators worldwide.
