Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CalcGrow and creator data.

How is the YouTube Revenue calculated?

Our engine uses a proprietary algorithm that multiplies daily views by industry-standard RPM (Revenue Per Mille) rates. You can adjust the RPM based on your niche (e.g., Finance pays higher than Entertainment) to get a highly accurate projection of AdSense earnings.

Do I need to link my Google Account?

Absolutely not. CalcGrow is a 100% independent and secure platform. We use public API data to fetch metrics. We never ask for your passwords, login credentials, or private analytics access.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, the core utility tools on CalcGrow are currently free for creators globally. Our goal is to empower digital entrepreneurs with transparent data without subscription paywalls.
