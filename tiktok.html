TikTok Monetization Guide 2026

The Creator Fund vs. Creativity Program

TikTok has shifted its focus from the original Creator Fund to the **Creativity Program Premier**. This new system pays significantly higher RPM (Revenue Per Mille), sometimes up to $1.00 per 1,000 qualified views, but only for videos longer than 60 seconds. Our calculator uses these updated 2026 metrics to give you a realistic estimate.

How to Maximize Your Earnings

  • Watch Time is King: TikTok's algorithm prioritizes retention. Use our Script Generator to create powerful "Hooks" in the first 3 seconds.
  • Regional RPM: Views from the USA, UK, and Germany pay significantly more than other regions.
  • Engagement: Likes and Shares don't pay directly, but they push your video to the FYP (For You Page), increasing your reach.
